FAQ

Questions we’re often asked

How do I know if coaching is right for me?

If you’re established, busy, and frustrated that your business still leans on you for almost everything, coaching is often the fastest way to shift that. The first call is simply to see if we’re the right partner — there’s no obligation to continue.

Do you work online or in person?

Most ongoing work is done online so we can meet consistently without travel time. For intensives or key sessions, in-person options may be available depending on location.

Will this add more to my already full plate?

Our focus is on simplifying, not adding complexity. We prioritise a few high-impact changes at a time so you see progress without feeling like you’ve taken on another job.

How long do clients typically work with you?

It varies, but many owners work with us for 3–9 months. Intensives can create significant change faster, while ongoing coaching supports you as new challenges and opportunities appear.

What does a discovery call look like?

We’ll talk through where you’re at, what’s keeping you stuck, and what “firing yourself from the day-to-day” would look like in your world. You’ll leave with clarity on options, whether we work together or not.

Do you work with my leadership team as well?

Yes, where it makes sense. Many engagements involve key team members so the changes don’t just live in your head — they become how the whole business works.
